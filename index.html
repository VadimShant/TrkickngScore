How it works

  • Find your trick, transition or variation
    (hook, tdr, st, cork, ironman, snapu, double, split)

  • After each trick, indicate the transition to the next element.
    (punch, pop, swingThrough, frontSwing, vanish, wt, carryThrough, etc.)

  • List trick variations separated by commas.
    (.., cart, punch, fulltwist, shuriken, twist, feilong, knife, ...)

  • Be honest when adding the execution quality coefficient.
    The highest score is a performance that cannot be faulted.
